Apple Pie by Grandma Ople



How long will it take?

  • Prep Time: 30mins

  • Cook Time: 1hr

  • Servings: 8

  • Yield: 1(9-inch) pie

  • Total Time: 1 hr 30 mins

Ingredients Needed

  • 8 Granny Smith Apples

  • Butter and Flour

  • White and Brown sugar

  • Use store-bought double crust pie pastry or make your own crust at home.

Steps to Deliciousness

  1. Make the filling
    • On the stove, make paste with butter and flour. Add water and sugar to help it broil. Simmer then remove from heat.

  2. Assemble the pie
    • Mold one crust into pie plate. Place sliced apples on the bottom crust. Use the top to make a lattice crust. Pour butter and sugar mixture over the lattice crust.

  3. Bake the Pie
    • Bake the pie in a preheated oven until the apple are soft and crust is golden. Baking will bake at 425 degrees F for 15 minutes, then reduce temperature to 350 degrees F for 35-45 minutes.

What to do when you are done


Put Pie in foil tightly and store and room temperature for up to two days, or in the fridge for up to four days.
