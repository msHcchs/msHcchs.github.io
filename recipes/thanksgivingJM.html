This is my Recipe for Rolls


Here are the ingredients needed:

-Flour 3 cups
-Yeast 1 tbsp
sugar 1/3 cup
-salt 1 tsp (use 1/2 tsp if using salted butter)
butter 1/3 cup
Milk 1 cup
1 egg

Video

Step by Step

  1. Make a Dough (recipe youtube.com)
  2. Knead the dough
  3. Cover the dough and let it rise
  4. Punch the air out and shape into balls
  5. Let rise again (for about 1 hour)
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes

This is the best recipe because everyone loves rolls. You can put whatever on a roll. Butter, jam, honey... Anything. Every family dinner I have the rolls always are gone by the end of it.
