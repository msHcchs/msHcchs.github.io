Pomegranate Salad

(Whipped Cream)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups of pomegranate seeds
  • Any other fruit you want to add

Instructions

  1. Add whipping cream and sugar into bowl
  2. Whisk until whipped thick
  3. Set aside
  4. Take pomegranates and cut upen to reveal the seeds
  5. Carefully peel out the seeds into a container until you've aquired the desired amount
  6. Add any other fruits with the pomegranates
  7. Mix together whipped cream and fruit

Why Is This The Best Recipe?

In my opinion, whipped cream pomegranate salad is the best Thanksgiving recipe because it's sweet, the pomegranates give it a little of a sour taste. I would say the only bad thing about pomegranate salad is that once you start eating, you can't stop. It's just that good.
