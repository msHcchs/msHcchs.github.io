Jays Banana Cream Pie

Welcome!

Here is a guide on how to make my favorite dessert- the famous banana cream pie.

Ingredients

  • 1 3.4- ounce package INSTANT vanilla pudding
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 14- ounce can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 8- serving graham cracker or cookie crusts
  • Lots of bananas 4-5 small bananas or 2-3 large ones
  • 1 pint heavy whipping cream
  • 1/3 cup powdered sugar

Image by: Sara Wells, https://ourbestbites.com/banana-cream-pie/

Now that you have your ingredients, let's make the pies.

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine one cup cold water, one package instant vanilla pudding, and one can sweetened condensed milk. whisk thouroughly until completely combined. Then, set it inside the fridge for a few moments to chill.
  2. Using an electric mixer, mix the heavy whipping cream until light peaks form. Set aside all but one cup and store in the fridge for later. Take the extra cup and dollop it in with the vanilla pudding mixture from Step 1.
  3. Cut slightly thick slices of banana and line the bottom of the two crusts. Take the completed filling mixture and evenly distribute between the crusts. Then, put the lids on them and set them in the fridge for several hours.
  4. Once the pies have chilled, begin by prepping the final layer. Take the leftover whipped cream from Step 2 and mix in 1/3 cup powdered sugar. Once completed, evenly distrubute between the pies.
  5. Add graham cracker sprinkle and bananas on top for a little extra touch (optional).

Tips

  • Mix the water and the pudding first, then add the sweetened condensed milk once combined. This will help remove the random chunks of unmixed pudding powder.
  • Make sure the pies chill for a good amount of time or the crust will fall apart and the flavor will not be as banana-y.
  • Don't forget the powdered sugar or the top layer of cream won't have that touch of sweetness!
