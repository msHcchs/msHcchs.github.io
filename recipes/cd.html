Pesto Pasta Recipe

A random pesto recipe that I found, looks pretty good.

Homepage

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pasta (penne, spaghetti, take your pick)
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts (Don't use walnuts or anything else as an alternative, it completley changes the taste of the dish)
  • 2/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese (this could get expensive, but the more expensive, the better pesto you're gonna have)
  • 2 garlic cloves, diced
  • Salt and pepper, personal prefrence
  • Juice of 1 lemon (optional, for added brightness)

Directions

  1. Cook the Pasta:Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook it however long you want, I prefer al dente (Usually 8-10 minutes).
  2. Make the Pesto: While the pasta cooks, place the basil leaves, pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, garlic, and your salt in the food blender. Pulse until it looks like a green sauce, not completley liquid.
  3. Add Olive Oil: After the other ingredients are blended, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the pesto reaches a smooth and creamy consistency. If its thick, good. Pesto is supposed to be more of a sauce thats kinda thick. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Combine the Pasta and Pesto: Toss the cooked pasta with the pesto sauce
  5. Buon Giorno: Eat the pesto pasta with extra grated Parmesan, and add fresh basil leaves (if you want, they're pretty expensive).
