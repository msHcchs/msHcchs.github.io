Reverse Seared Steak On a Traeger



Prep Time
5 Min		 Cook Time
15 Min		 Pellet Type
Pecan

My family started doing steak for thanksgiving when year after year the turkey would go untouched. Most people in my family don't like turkey and steak is what everyone wanted. The next year we did steak and turkey and again the turkey went untouched and everyone wanted more steak. Now my family only does steak.


Prep

Ingrediants

  • Steak
  • Traeger Rub

Prep Directions

  1. Heat traeger too 425 degrees.
  2. Apply the traeger rub to the steak and spread evenly.

Grilling

Grilling Directions

  1. Once traeger is heated put steaks on the grill.
  2. Cook until desired temperature.
Internal Temperature Result
120 Degrees Rare
130 Degrees Medium Rare
140 Degrees Medium
145 Degrees Medium-Well
155 Degrees Well Done

Reverse Sear

Reverse Sear Directions

  1. Put the steak on a cast iron pan with butter to sear.
  2. Sear the steak for 3 minutes on each side.
  3. Take of the pan and let the steak rest for 5 minutes.
  4. Enjoy!
