Pumpkin Pie

Pumpkin pie has always been a family favorite for my household! Every year, me and my family get together to make this amazing pumpkin pie recipe. I would now like to share this recipe with you!

Ingrediants

For the crust:

  1. 1 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  3. 1/2 cup cold butter or shortening
  4. 3 tablespoons cold water

For the filling:

  1. 2 cups cooked pumpkin
  2. 1 can evaported milk
  3. 2 large eggs
  4. 3/4 brown sugar
  5. 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  6. 1/2 teaspoon ginger
  7. 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  8. 1/2 teaspoon salt

How to make

  1. Mix the flour and salt. Cut in the shortening, then add the water.
  2. Shape the dough into a ball. Roll it out on a lightly floured surface.
  3. Cut the rolled dough and fit it into the pie pan.
  4. Beat the filling ingredients together, then pour it into the prepared crust.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven until a knife comes out clean.
