Maci's Cornbread Recipe

Steve Johnson, Cornbread muffins, 2010, site found

Ingrediants needed:

  • 1 1/4 cups of all purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup of yellow cornmeal
  • 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • 1 stick (1/2 cup) of unsalted butter

Instructions

Step 1:

Get ready by getting all your ingrediants and utensils ready and preheat the oven to 350 degrees

Step 2:

whisk the eggs in a medium sized bowl

Step 3:

Add in the honey and milk to the eggs and whisk to combine

Step 4:

In a seperate larger bowl combine the flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, and salt (whisk to mix)

Step 5:

Add in the egg mixture and the melted butter to the dry ingrediants

Step 6:

Mix thuroughly until the dry and wet ingrediants are fully combined

Step 7:

Coat your muffin pan with butter to get a buttery crisp to the outside of your muffins and to make them easier to remove

Step 8:

Spoon the batter into a muffin pan, filling each cup about 3/4 full

Step 9:

Toss them in the oven for about 17-20 minuted or until tops are set and golden

Step 10:

Once the muffins are out of the oven let them cool for for about 3 minutes, then enjoy the delicous muffins

Why should you make this dish?

If you want a delicous addition to your thanksgiving feast, or even just a easy treat to make
on a cool fall night, this is the dish for you! A easy recipe that anyone can do and have a delicous reward at the end. Cornbread is the perfect side dish for Thanksgiving and the whole table will love it. It was always my favorite side on thanksgiving and I think you'll love it too!
