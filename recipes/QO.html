This is my Recipe for Brussel Sprouts

The Ingredients:
-4 quarts of water with 1 1/2 tbsp kosher salt
-1/2 tsp kosher salt
-1/3 cup olive oil
-2 1/4 lbs Brussel sprouts

Video

Step by Step Recipe

  1. Trim the sprouts
  2. Boil the sprouts
  3. Strain the sprouts
  4. Sheet the pan
  5. Cover the brussel sprouts in oil
  6. Serve and Enjoy

Why these are the best choice

This recipe is the best choice because, brussel sprouts are a healthy thing to eat at thanksgiving. I honestly love baked brussel sprouts. They are super juicy and flavorful. Brussel sprouts are also super fun to make, either with friends or family.
